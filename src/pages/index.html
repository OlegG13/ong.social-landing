Wizebbit Vision

WizeBit is a blockchain technology company that specializes in software, hardware and AI. As part of our services we are developing a community-driven marketplace providing applications, technologies and content that will reward, create and expand the first Personal Assistant on blockchain.  

WizeBit’s focuses on software development of a private blockchain prioritizes data sharding and hash compressing algorithms. This will determine the type of peer-to-peer management, improve areas of resistance to modification of data, enhance efficiency of recorded transactions and increase protocol layers for validation, authentication and security.   

WizeBit Blockchain will be the foundation to building enterprise software solutions with decentralize cloud computing. Ensuring transactions are secure and redundant while providing low latency data throughput. WizeBit will integrate process with an encrypted channel providing seamless security and data protection on our network and devices.

is your home safe?

WizeBox is design to act as your master device connecting all of your smart endpoints securely and seamlessly. WizeBox will also connect to third-party applications to expand her range of services, creating a true “Internet of Experiences" not Things.  
The interaction of your WizeBox with peripheral devices in your home is implemented through the use of cryptography systems, secure data transfer protocols and also, if an external device allows, encoding of transmitted data using algorithms RSA, SHA-256 and AES-256.  
WizeBox adopts blockchain’s cryptographic functions, permitting access and transparency for purposes of innovation, monitoring and careful documentation while rendering the actual data sets more resistant to tampering than currently known technology.

CONTROL SMART DEVICES
The WizeBox will run your local version of the blockchain, along with a set of associated smart contacts. This will enable the WizeBox to receive data from the devices in your home, store the history of this data safely and also control the state of the connected devices.    
Visual Home Mapping 
User interaction with the WizeBox will be done through on the device with an easy to use dashboard. A tab on your WizeBox dashboard will display a map of all devices in your home, as well as their current state. This data is sourced from your home’s local blockchain and is then displayed to you in real time. Alerts can be set based upon the status of connected devices.     
Machine learning
 The WizeBox constantly queries connected devices and performs analytical calculations from the collected data. The WizeBox dashboard will display these results to you as a separate tab of home reports. A separate AI module giving the specific WizeBox the ability to learn your patterns and perform behavior modeling. This will allow the WizeBox to affirmatively assist you in managing all system components of your on-premise automation and on-premise security.

WizeBit’s ecosystem will enable all innovative ideas to see the light of day through Community Consensus. The WizeBit platform will allow any community member to submit an innovative idea at any time. The community either upvotes or downvotes the suggested idea.
